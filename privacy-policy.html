Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you access and use our course website. By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Policy.

Information We Collect:

a. Personal Information: When you register for an account or enroll in a course, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, contact information, and payment details. We only collect information necessary to provide our services and improve your learning experience.

b. Usage Information: We may collect non-personal information about your interactions with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages visited. This information helps us analyze trends, administer the site, and improve our services.

Use of Information:

a. We use your personal information to: Provide access to courses, content, and resources you have enrolled in. Communicate with you regarding course updates, notifications, and administrative purposes. Process payments for course enrollment and handle refunds or cancellations. Personalize your learning experience and recommend relevant courses based on your interests.

We may use non-personal information for analytical purposes, website improvement, and marketing initiatives.

Information Sharing:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

b. We may share your personal information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and delivering our services, such as payment processors or course facilitators. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

c. We may disclose personal information if required by law, to protect our rights, or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process.

Data Security:

a. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or alteration.

b. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us, and we will take appropriate steps to remove the information.
